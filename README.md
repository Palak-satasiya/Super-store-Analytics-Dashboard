Developed an interactive Super Store Analytics Dashboard using Tableau to analyze retail sales and profitability. The dashboard includes KPI cards for Total Sales and Profit, a donut chart for category-wise sales, a bar chart for sub-category sales, a monthly sales and profit trend analysis, and a category-wise profit comparison. Interactive filters for Category and Sub-Category allow users to explore the data dynamically. The dashboard helps identify top-performing products, profitable categories, seasonal sales trends, and business opportunities. This project enhanced my skills in data visualization, dashboard design, calculated fields, filters, parameters, and business intelligence, enabling effective data-driven decision-making through clear and interactive reporting.
